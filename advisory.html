Oversight

Overview

In 2019, Limestone Capital established it’s first ever Advisory Board to help manage our increasingly complex operations as well as strengthen the connection between our extensive alumni base and current members. The Board will consist of five Limestone alumni, with one chair person, two senior members (three or more years of industry experience), and two junior members (less than three years of industry experience). Furthermore, the board will be split into two separate committees in the form of a strategic Committee and an Investment Committee. Each committee will consist of one junior board member, one senior board member and two current executive members with the providing oversight to both arms.

The Investment Committee

Limestone’s Investment Committee is centered around the club’s investment-related tasks to optimize the performance of our Canadian, U.S, and Opportunistic portfolios. These efforts are fully encompassing to help our internal investment processes by continually augmenting our technical education, internal modelling practices, and overall investment philosophy while also strengthening our fundraising efforts.

The Strategic Committee

Limestone’s Strategic Committee mainly supports our current members’ recruiting efforts in order to help them successfully attain top-tier career opportunities in finance, consulting, and technology. Additionally, the Strategic Committee aims to enhance how we govern our current members and overall operations, including internal and external events, member engagement, and the stock selection process.
